Known Limitations & Assumptions
  • Non-composite sections only — Mcr is calculated assuming no staged construction. The term Mdnc·(Sc/Snc − 1) in AASHTO 5.6.3.3-1 is taken as zero (Sc = Snc).
  • Bonded tendons only — γ₂ = 1.1 per Table 5.6.3.3-1 for bonded tendons. Unbonded tendons (γ₂ = 1.0) are not considered.
  • Single cross-section analysis — The app analyzes a single section at a time. Span-level effects, development lengths, and anchorage zone checks are not performed.
  • Appendix B5 convergence — The iterative B5 method may not converge for all strain/geometry combinations. When convergence fails, B5 results are reported as N/A.
  • Longitudinal reinforcement check (5.7.3.5) — Based on user judgment whether to apply. Not automatically enabled/disabled by section classification.
  • Torsion — Torsional resistance follows the thin-walled tube analogy per AASHTO 5.7.3.6. The equivalent thin-walled tube is approximated from the gross section geometry.
  • PT effective prestress (fpe) — Used directly as input or computed from the PT Design tab loss model. Long-term creep, shrinkage, and relaxation losses should be independently verified.
  • fcpe for Mcr — Computed as Pe/Ag + Pe·e·yt/Ig using gross section properties. Transformed section effects are neglected.
